Capital Return Framework

Banking Sector Context
Banks like Cathay General Bancorp return capital to shareholders through two primary mechanisms:

1. Cash Dividends: Regular quarterly distributions representing a percentage of earnings
2. Share Repurchases: Buyback programs authorized by the Board to reduce share count and enhance per-share metrics

Key metrics for evaluating capital return include:
‚Ä¢ Dividend Payout Ratio: Dividends √∑ Net Income (current 32.1% within 30-40%)

‚Ä¢ Total Shareholder Return: Dividend Yield 3.31% + Share Price Appreciation
