Data Quality Issues Detail

PPBI - Duration/Instant Tag Mismatch

Issue
SEC companyfacts equity tags mix instant/duration values, yielding TBVPS $0.01 (obviously erroneous for $24.49 stock).
Impact: ROTE -262%, P/TBV 2,959x (garbage outputs)
Resolution: Requires manual 10-Q parsing to extract correct equity components. Excluded from regression.

BANC - Negative Tangible Common Equity

Issue
Q2'25 Balance Sheet: Stockholders' equity $204.3M, Goodwill $197.8M, Intangibles $15.6M
Computed TCE: $204.3M - $197.8M - $15.6M = -$9.06M (NEGATIVE)
Impact: P/TBV and ROTE not computable (division by negative)
Interpretation: Intangible assets exceed total equity (typical of banks with M&A history or impaired goodwill). Not comparable to CATY (positive TCE $2,507M).

WAFD - Structural Discount

Issue
Valuation: P/TBV 0.37x despite ROTE 14.2% (healthy profitability)
Fitted Value: Regression implies P/TBV ~1.25x based on ROTE 14.2%
Discount: Actual 0.37x is 3.4x below fitted value (70% discount)
Interpretation: Market pricing WAFD at 63% discount to TBV suggests concerns about earnings quality, asset quality, or business model sustainability. This is structural outlier, not data error.
Note: SEC diagnostics confirmed TBVPS $75.91 is CORRECT (not tag error).

COLB - One-Time Gains

Issue
LTM ROTE: 33.43% (2.8x peer median 11.95%)
Quarterly NI Diagnostic: Q4'24 $425M spike, Q2'25 $378M, FY2024 $581M (vs typical quarters ~$140M)
Cook's Distance: 4.030 (destroying regression fit)
Impact: With COLB included (n=7), Adj R¬≤ = -0.077 (model worse than mean). Without COLB (n=4), Adj R¬≤ = 0.932 (strong fit).
Interpretation: Large quarterly spikes suggest non-recurring gains (securities sales, loan sale gains, merger items). ROTE 33% not representative of through-cycle profitability.
