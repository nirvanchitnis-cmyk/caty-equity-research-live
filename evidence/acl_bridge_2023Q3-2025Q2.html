LIMITATIONS & METHODOLOGY NOTES

  1. Q3'23 and Q4'23 estimates: Beginning balances derived by working backwards from confirmed Q1'24 and Q2'24 data. Provision and NCO figures estimated to achieve known ending balances. These are APPROXIMATIONS pending re-extraction from original filings.
  1. Average loans: Sourced from MD&A net interest margin tables when available. Earlier quarters estimated based on loan growth trajectory.
  1. Coverage ratio: Calculated as Ending ACL / Average Loans (not period-end loans) for consistency with earnings releases.
  1. NCO derivation: Where charge-off and recovery detail not extractable from XBRL, used accounting identity. This is GAAP-compliant but may mask composition (commercial vs. CRE vs. consumer).
