ℹ️ Calculation Methodology
Q2 2025 discrete quarter values computed as:
Q2 discrete = (6-month YTD value from Q2 10-Q) − (Q1 three-month value from Q1 10-Q)

Example:
• Net cash from ops (6M YTD 2025): $162.911M
• Net cash from ops (Q1 2025 discrete): $98.959M
• Q2 2025 discrete = $162.911M − $98.959M = $63.952M
