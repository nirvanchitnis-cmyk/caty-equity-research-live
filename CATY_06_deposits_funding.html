Noninterest-Bearing Trend

IMPROVING NIB Mix
Noninterest-bearing deposits moved from 16.9% in Q2 2025 to 17.4% in Q3 2025, a change of 0.5 ppts. Noninterest-bearing mix ticked higher sequentially following treasury stock repatriation and seasonal cash builds.
Long-term change versus Q1 2022: -6.6 ppts.
